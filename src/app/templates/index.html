Tracing and visualizing diachronic semantic change

What is Open SemShift?

Meaning of words evolves constantly in one language. Our tool can help you visualize the semantic shift of the word of your choice. We currently support English and German, with a set of more than 100 words in each language. More languages are coming soon!
